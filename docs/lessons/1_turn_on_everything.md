# 1. Turn on everything

To use the laser, one needs to turn on:

- The laser
- The ventilation
- The computer

## Exam

Preparation:

- Turn off the laser
- Turn off the ventilation
- Turn off the computer

Within 5 minutes:

- Turn on the laser
- Turn on the ventilation for two hours
- Turn on the computer
